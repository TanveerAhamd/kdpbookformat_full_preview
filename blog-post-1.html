How to Format Your Paperback for Amazon KDP

May 10, 2025 • 7 min read

Formatting a paperback for Amazon KDP requires correct trim size, margins, headers, footers, and a table of contents. Below is a practical checklist:

  • Choose the correct trim size in KDP.
  • Set appropriate margins and gutter for binding.
  • Ensure page numbers are correctly placed.
  • Embed fonts or use system fonts compatible with PDF/X standards.
  • Export to PDF following KDP requirements.
