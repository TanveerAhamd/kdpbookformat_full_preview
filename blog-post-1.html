Formatting your paperback for Amazon KDP requires precision and attention to detail. Margins, bleed, and trim size determine how your book appears in print, and any mistake can affect its professional appearance.

1. Set Up Your Document Correctly

Begin by choosing the correct trim size in Word or InDesign (e.g., 6x9 inches). Set margins between 0.5‚Äì0.75 inches, and remember to include mirror margins for the gutter.

2. Add Bleed and Ensure Proper Spacing

If your book includes images or backgrounds that reach the edge of the page, add a 0.125-inch bleed on all sides.

3. Export to PDF

Always export your final file as a print-ready PDF. Ensure all fonts are embedded and image resolution is 300 DPI.

Following these steps ensures your paperback looks clean, professional, and fully compliant with KDP print requirements.
