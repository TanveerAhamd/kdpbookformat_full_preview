1. Use Consistent Headings and Styles

Always apply heading styles (H1, H2, H3) instead of manual bolding to maintain consistent navigation.

2. Avoid Page Numbers

Kindle devices use location references instead of page numbers, so remove any page numbering from your manuscript.
