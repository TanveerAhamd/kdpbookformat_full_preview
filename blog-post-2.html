Essential Tips for Formatting Your eBook for Kindle

Apr 28, 2025 • 6 min read

eBook formatting requires clean HTML-like structure, no fixed page sizes, proper image sizing, and tested table of contents. Key tips:

  • Use semantic headings (H1, H2) and avoid complex page layouts.
  • Optimize images for file size and use correct resolution.
  • Test on multiple Kindle previewers and devices.
