Self-Publishing vs Traditional Publishing

📅 Feb 28, 2025 • ✍️ By Admin

Writers today have more publishing options than ever before. Choosing between self-publishing and traditional publishing depends on your goals, resources, and timeline.

1. Creative Control

Self-publishing via KDP gives you full control over your content, pricing, and marketing strategy — no approvals needed.

2. Royalties and Earnings

Self-published authors on KDP earn up to 70% royalties, compared to 10–15% in traditional publishing deals.

3. Speed to Market

With KDP, your book can be live within 72 hours, while traditional publishing can take 6–12 months or longer.

For most independent authors, KDP’s flexibility and higher earnings make self-publishing the ideal choice.
